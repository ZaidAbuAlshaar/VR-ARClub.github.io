About Our Club

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be a hub for innovation and creativity, empowering students to become leaders in the field of immersive technology. Our mission is to provide a supportive community, educational resources, and hands-on opportunities to develop a new generation of VR and AR creators.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of passionate students, the VR & AR Club started as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts with a shared dream. We quickly grew into a thriving community, organizing our first workshop on Unity and a mini-hackathon that brought together developers from across campus. We believe that immersive tech is not just a trend but a fundamental shift in how we interact with the world, and we're excited to be at the forefront of this change.
